Kemeshwar Singh Maharaj

Last Ruling Maharaj Of Darbhanga

Maharaja Sir Kameshwar Singh (1907–1962) was the last ruling Maharaja of the Darbhanga Raj
in Bihar, a prominent zamindari estate. A noted philanthropist, politician, and educationist,
he served in the Constituent Assembly of India and established institutions like Kameshwar
Singh Darbhanga Sanskrit University.
